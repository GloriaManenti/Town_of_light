The Town of Light

A Psychological Horror Game

The story

The main character, Renee, comes back to the asylum after having been a hospitalized there in the past. The player has to help Renee in remembering her past while interacting with the environment and some other characters. There are four different possibile endings of the game, depending your choices while playing.

The Idea

The idea of the game was developed in 2011, when the developers found out that near their Italian hometown there had been an asylum, one of the biggest of all Italy. The place seemed immediately the perfect setting for a videogame, and the game ends up being also an historical documentary based on real happenings. The result of a very creative process, Town of light is an innovative game, which combines a personal experience told in first person with a realistic setting. Instead of being surrounded by monsters and dangers, the player experiences the same feelings of terror and despair patients felt inside the former psychiatric hospital a century ago.

Reviewers say

The game is very suggestive and raises aware of mental illnesses. The programmers wanted to raise awareness of depression and the result is not the traditional horror game, but it is much more than that: it is a complete gameplay experience that allows major changes in the main story and different possible endings.
